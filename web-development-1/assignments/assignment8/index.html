Overwatch Classic

Overwatch Classic is a gamemode in Overwatch 2 available from 12 November 2024 to 2 December 2024. This mode puts players into 6v6 combat featuring only the heroes and maps from the release of Overwatch 1. All of the heroes have had their abilities restored to their first versions. This is the first time that Overwatch 2 has had an offical gamemode with 6 players on each team. Only the Overwatch 1 default skins are available to use, and the HUD icons feature the Overwatch 1 art. There are plans to bring other patches to the game, including during the Moth Meta and GOATS eras.
